At AsookeFashion, we believe African fashion deserves a place on the global stage. Our collections combine the richness of traditional Aṣọ Òkè craftsmanship with clean, modern tailoring — clothing that is timeless, elegant, and effortlessly wearable.

Handwoven · Lagos · Worldwide
01 — Our story

Founded by Olaide Samuel Okediji, an Oyo prince with a deep passion for preserving the rich cultural heritage of the Oyo Kingdom through fashion.

AsookeFashion was created to celebrate African craftsmanship while redefining luxury for the modern woman. Every design is made for women who appreciate premium fabrics, exceptional craftsmanship, and versatile pieces that transition seamlessly from work to weddings, celebrations, travel, and everyday elegance.

We collaborate with experienced artisans and skilled designers to transform one of Africa’s most iconic textiles into contemporary fashion that celebrates heritage without compromising comfort, sophistication, or quality. From statement dresses and coordinated sets to elegant kaftans, jumpsuits, and occasion wear, every collection reflects confidence, individuality, and timeless style.

02 — Our mission

To make authentic African luxury fashion accessible to women around the world — while preserving the beauty, craftsmanship, and legacy of traditional Aṣọ Òkè.

Whether you’re shopping for a wedding, special occasion, vacation, office wear, or everyday elegance, AsookeFashion offers premium African fashion designed to transcend trends and remain stylish for years to come.
